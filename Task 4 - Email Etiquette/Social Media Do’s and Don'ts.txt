Social Media Do’s

Use strong and unique passwords for all social media accounts.
Use professional platforms (like linkedin) to build a digital presence .
Think carefully before posting photos, comments, or personal details.
Do fact check information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.
Report harmful or suspicious content when you notice .



Social Media Don’ts

Do not share personal information such as passwords, phone numbers ,etc.
Do not accept friend requests from unknown people without verifying their identity.
Do not click on suspicious links that may contain harmful content or scams.
Do not use social media during class or meetings.
Do not download files or apps from unknown links.
