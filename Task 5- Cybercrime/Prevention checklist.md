#### **Title: UPI Fraud Prevention Checklist**



1\. Always verify the identity of the person requesting payment before approving any UPI request.



2\. Never scan QR codes sent by unknown people, as scanning a QR code usually sends money instead of receiving it.



3\. Do not share OTPs, UPI PINs, or bank details with anyone, even if they claim to be bank officials.



4\. Carefully read payment notifications before approving any transaction to avoid accidental money transfer.



5\. Use secure and updated payment applications and avoid installing apps from unknown sources.



6\. Regularly check bank transaction history to quickly identify any unauthorized payments.



7\. If you become a victim of online payment fraud, immediately report the incident to the National Cyber Crime Portal.



8\. In case of financial fraud, contact the cybercrime helpline number 1930 as soon as possible to report the issue and block further transactions.
