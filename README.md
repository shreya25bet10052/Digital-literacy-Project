## Project Overview

This Digital Literacy Project was completed to develop practical knowledge of digital tools, professional communication, and cyber safety awareness. The goal of this project was to move beyond just using technology and instead focus on mastering "Digital Literacy" as a professional skill. 

---

## Tasks Completed

### Task 1: Infographic Creation

In this task, I have created a digital infographic using the tool Canva to explain important digital literacy concepts. The infographic was designed using proper layout, colors, and icons to make the content visually clear and easy to understand.

---

### Task 2: LinkedIn Profile Development

In this task, I have created and updated my profile on professional platforms like LinkedIn , GitHub and Kaggle.In this Academic details, skills, and interests were added to build a strong digital identity and understand the importance of professional networking.

---

### Task 3: Digital Skills Development

**Part A: Coding Practice Platform**
In this part, a coding practice platform was used to solve programming problems and improve logical thinking skills. This activity helped in understanding how coding platforms can enhance problem-solving abilities and strengthen programming fundamentals.

**Part B: Google Form Quiz Creation**
In this part, a Google Form quiz was created based on digital awareness topics. Multiple-choice questions were designed to test knowledge of digital safety and responsible online behavior.

### Task 4: Communication and Social Media Awareness

**Part A: Email Writing**
This part involved writing formal emails such as extension requests and internship inquiries. It helped in learning proper email structure, professional language, and formal communication etiquette.

**Part B: Social Media Do’s and Don’ts**
In this part, a checklist of Social Media Do’s and Don’ts was created to promote responsible online behavior. This included guidelines for protecting personal information and maintaining respectful communication on social platforms.

---

### Task 5: Cybercrime Awareness

**Part A: Cybercrime Case Study**
A case study based on UPI payment fraud was prepared to understand how cybercrimes occur. The case study explained how fraud typically happens, who is targeted, and the possible consequences.

**Part B: Prevention Checklist**
A cybercrime prevention checklist was created to list important safety measures. The checklist included tips to avoid UPI fraud and information about reporting cybercrime through official helplines.
